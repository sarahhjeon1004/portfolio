✨ AI Summary
Based on 18 research items across Dovetail, Analytics, and UPF Hub:
(1) Enterprise customers express cautious optimism about AI features but want transparency in how AI-generated outputs are produced.
(2) 67% of enterprise users have tried AI features at least once, but only 23% use them weekly.
(3) Trust is the top barrier — users want to verify AI outputs before sharing with clients.
(4) The Customer Council identified that workflow integration matters more than feature count.
(5) UPF Hub sentiment data confirms this: AI-related feedback sentiment is 32% negative across enterprise accounts, with "lack of explainability" as the top-tagged theme.
